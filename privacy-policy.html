At WishYou.ai, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

When you create a birthday card on WishYou.ai, we collect:

  • Card content — the name, message, sign-off, and song you enter when creating a card
  • Photos — images you upload are stored securely on Cloudinary (a third-party image hosting service)
  • Usage data — how many times a card has been viewed (view count), stored in Firebase
  • Your name (sender) — if you choose to include it on the card

We do not collect your email address, phone number, or create user accounts.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and display your birthday card to the recipient
  • To store your card temporarily (cards expire after 3 days)
  • To count how many times your card has been opened
  • To improve our AI-powered features (via Anthropic's Claude API)

3. Data Storage & Retention

Card data (text, photos, and metadata) is stored in Firebase Realtime Database and Cloudinary. Cards automatically expire and are deleted after 3 days from creation. We do not store any data beyond this period unless you upgrade to a paid plan.

4. Third-Party Services

WishYou.ai uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — for card data storage
  • Cloudinary — for photo storage
  • Anthropic Claude API — for AI-powered card personalisation and song suggestions
  • YouTube Data API — for music search
  • Cloudflare Workers — for secure API proxying

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

5. Photos & Media

Photos you upload are stored on Cloudinary servers and are accessible via a unique URL embedded in your card link. Once your card expires (after 3 days), the photos associated with that card are no longer served. We do not use your photos for any other purpose.

6. Cookies

WishYou.ai does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We do not run ads.

7. Children's Privacy

WishYou.ai is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal accounts, there is no login to delete. If you wish to request deletion of a specific card before it auto-expires, please contact us at hello@wishyou.ai with your card link.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
